Yourbit Rewards


What are Yourbit Rewards?

Yourbit rewards is a program created to help encourage users to engage more with their content. Higher user engagement can be helpful for everyone on the platform. More interaction data leads to better reccomendation and more insights for creators. Yourbit rewards also provides a way for creators to boost themselves regardless of existing popularity or financial status.

How it works

Every time you provide feedback on a post that you haven't yet provided feedback on, you will recieve rewards points, the amount depends on the action. You can redeem your rewards points for boost perks on your profile, community, or on a specific post. Boost perks may vary depending on what scope you choose to promote at.
