Yourbit Child Safety and CSAE Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

At Yourbit, the safety and wellbeing of our users—especially children—is our highest priority. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. This includes content, behavior, or interactions that involve or promote the sexual exploitation or endangerment of minors.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

Yourbit maintains a zero tolerance stance toward any form of CSAE content or conduct. We do not allow:

  • Content that depicts, promotes, or attempts to solicit child sexual abuse
  • Grooming, predatory behavior, or inappropriate communication with minors
  • Use of the platform to exploit, harm, or endanger children in any way

Any violations will result in immediate account termination and legal reporting.

2. Moderation and Detection

We use a combination of:

  • Automated detection tools to scan for known illegal content
  • User reporting systems to allow anyone to flag abuse
  • Manual review by trained staff to investigate concerns

Suspicious or reported content is reviewed and actioned promptly.

3. Reporting CSAE Content

If you encounter any content or behavior that may be related to CSAE, report it immediately by:

  • Using the in-app “Report” button
  • Emailing us at: abuse@yourbit.com

Reports are treated with urgency and confidentiality.

4. Law Enforcement Cooperation

Yourbit cooperates with law enforcement agencies and relevant organizations including:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Local, state, and federal authorities (as required by U.S. law)

We preserve evidence, disable accounts, and submit reports when CSAE activity is suspected.

5. Commitment to Ongoing Safety

Yourbit continuously reviews and improves its safety policies and technologies. We are committed to building a safe, responsible platform for all users.

Need Help?

If you have questions or urgent safety concerns, please contact us at support@yourbit.com or visit our Help Center.
